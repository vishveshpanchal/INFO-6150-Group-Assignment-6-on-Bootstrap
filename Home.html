Paneer Tikka

  • 1. Gather the following ingredients: paneer cubes, yogurt, gram flour (besan), ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, lemon juice, and salt.
  • 2. In a bowl, mix yogurt, gram flour, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, lemon juice, and salt to make a smooth marinade.
  • 3. Dip the paneer cubes in the marinade, ensuring they are well coated, and let them marinate for at least 30 minutes..
  • 4. Thread the marinated paneer cubes onto skewers and grill them in an oven or on a barbecue until they are charred and cooked through.
  • 5. Serve the Paneer Tikka hot, garnished with lemon wedges and accompanied by mint chutney.
